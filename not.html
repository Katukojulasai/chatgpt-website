How Not to Use ChatGPT

Avoid these common pitfalls:

  • Sole Source: Don't rely on ChatGPT as the only source of information.
  • Sensitive Data: Avoid sharing personal or confidential information.
  • Unethical Use: Do not use ChatGPT for malicious purposes, such as generating spam or misinformation.
  • Plagiarism: Do not directly copy and paste generated text without proper attribution.
  • Automated Decision-Making: Avoid using ChatGPT for critical automated decision-making without human oversight.

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a large language model developed by OpenAI, capable of understanding and generating human-like text. It's trained on a massive dataset of text and code, allowing it to perform various tasks such as answering questions, writing essays, summarizing text, and generating creative content. **What is ChatGPT?

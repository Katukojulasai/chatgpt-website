How to Use ChatGPT

To effectively use ChatGPT, follow these guidelines:

  • Be Specific: Provide clear and detailed prompts.
  • Iterate: Refine your prompts based on the model's responses.
  • Context: Maintain context by continuing the conversation.
  • Experiment: Try different phrasing and styles.
  • Verify: Double-check critical information, as ChatGPT can sometimes generate incorrect or misleading responses.
